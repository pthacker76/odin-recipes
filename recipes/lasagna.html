Lasagna

Best Lasagna Ever

Here is a recipe for the best lasagna on the planet!

Ingredients

  • noodles
  • sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Italian Seasoning

Instructions

  1. Preheat Oven to 450 degrees
  2. Arrange noodles, sauce, ricotta, mozz, seasoning in 3 layers
  3. Add more mozz
  4. Cook for 40 minutes covered with tin-foil
  5. Cook additional 10 minutes uncovered
