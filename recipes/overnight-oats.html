Overnight Oats

Best Overnight Oats in the world

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup of Old Fashioned Oats (not steel-cut)
  • 1/2 Cup of Milk
  • 1 Tablespoon Maple Syrup
  • Berries,Nuts,Seeds

Instructions

  1. Combine oats,syrup and milk in a jar
  2. Leave sealed jar in fridge overnight
  3. Put toppings on, stir and enjoy
